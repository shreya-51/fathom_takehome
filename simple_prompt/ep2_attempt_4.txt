Here are the story arcs from the script:

1. Label: Ryder Cup Team Declines White House Invitation
   Description: The Ryder Cup team declines the White House's invitation to visit, due to a joke the President made. This becomes a major issue that the White House staff has to deal with.
   Characters: President Bartlet, Josh, Toby, C.J.
   Themes: Humor, public relations, political fallout

2. Label: Vice President Hoynes' Strained Relationship with the White House
   Description: There is tension between Vice President Hoynes and the White House, particularly Leo, over Hoynes' comments and actions. This threatens to become a larger political conflict.
   Characters: Vice President Hoynes, Leo, C.J.
   Themes: Intra-administration tensions, political maneuvering

3. Label: Sam's Encounter with a Call Girl
   Description: Sam accidentally sleeps with a call girl named Laurie, and struggles with how to handle the situation, seeking advice from Josh and Toby.
   Characters: Sam, Josh, Toby, Laurie
   Themes: Ethics, personal conduct, discretion

4. Mandy's Unemployment and Recruitment by the White House
   Description: Mandy, the former media consultant, is recruited by the White House to help with their public relations issues, despite some objections from Josh.
   Characters: Mandy, Josh, Toby, Leo
   Themes: Staffing decisions, team dynamics, professional redemption

5. President Bartlet Deals with the Tragic Death of Dr. Tolliver
   Description: President Bartlet is devastated to learn that Dr. Tolliver, the White House physician, has been killed in a terrorist attack. This leads to a serious national security crisis that the President must address.
   Characters: President Bartlet, Leo
   Themes: Tragedy, national security, presidential decision-making
